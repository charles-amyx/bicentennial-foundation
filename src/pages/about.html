About Us

About Us

The Michigan Engineering Bicentennial Web Project is a multimedia story collection intended to:

  • Engage our Michigan Engineering community in compelling stories that reflect upon and draw meaning from our productive past;
  • Showcase our long and deep commitment to educating future leaders, discovering and employing new technologies, and finding solutions that significantly impact the world’s most pressing needs and challenges;
  • Bring prior Michigan Engineering communities back to vivid life for the examination, education, and entertainment of current and future Michigan Engineering communities; and
  • Connect our most meaningful achievements in ways that stimulate conversations about likely future breakthroughs.

This Project is produced by Michigan Engineering's Office of Communications and Marketing, in association with the Office of the Dean, the Office of Advancement and the College's academic units (and their communications specialists), with special assistance from the Bentley Historical Library.

The Michigan Engineering Bicentennial Web Project is dedicated to everyone who has contributed to Michigan Engineering’s significant history – and to all those who will.
